Lasagna

Lasagna, a quintessential Italian dish, is a layered and comforting culinary masterpiece that has found its way into the hearts and homes of people worldwide. This savory delight is constructed with alternating layers of tender lasagna noodles, rich and flavorful meat sauce, creamy ricotta cheese, and a generous sprinkling of melted mozzarella and Parmesan. The assembly of these components creates a harmonious symphony of textures and tastes, capturing the essence of comfort food. The sauce, often a robust blend of ground meat, tomatoes, herbs, and spices, infuses each layer with a hearty, savory goodness. Whether enjoyed on a cozy family dinner table or at festive gatherings, lasagna stands as a symbol of warmth, tradition, and the timeless joy derived from indulging in a satisfying, well-crafted meal.

Ingredients List

  • 1 pound (450g) ground beef
  • 1/2 pound (225g) Italian sausage, removed from casings
  • 1 onion, finely chopped
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 can (28 ounces/800g) crushed tomatoes
  • 2 cans (14 ounces/400g each) tomato sauce
  • 2 cans (6 ounces/170g each) tomato paste
  • 1/2 cup red wine (optional)
  • 2 teaspoons dried basil
  • 2 teaspoons dried oregano
  • 1 teaspoon sugar
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 12 lasagna noodles, cooked according to package instructions
  • 3 cups ricotta cheese
  • 2 cups shredded mozzarella cheese
  • 1 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • 1 egg
  • 2 tablespoons fresh parsley, chopped

Instructions

  1. In a large skillet, brown the ground beef and Italian sausage over medium heat. Add chopped onions and garlic, and cook until onions are translucent.
  2. Stir in crushed tomatoes, tomato sauce, tomato paste, and red wine (if using). Add basil, oregano, sugar, salt, and pepper. Simmer for 30-45 minutes, stirring occasionally.
  3. In a bowl, combine ricotta cheese, 1 1/2 cups mozzarella cheese, Parmesan cheese, egg, and chopped parsley. Mix well.
  4. Preheat your oven to 375°F (190°C).
  5. In a 9x13-inch baking dish, spread a thin layer of the meat sauce. Place a layer of cooked lasagna noodles on top.
  6. Spread half of the ricotta cheese mixture over the noodles. Top with a portion of the meat sauce. Repeat the layers, finishing with a layer of meat sauce on top.
  7. Sprinkle the remaining mozzarella and Parmesan cheese on top.
  8. Cover the baking dish with aluminum foil and bake in the preheated oven for 25 minutes. Remove the foil and bake for an additional 10-15 minutes, or until the cheese is melted and bubbly.
  9. Allow the lasagna to rest for 10 minutes before slicing. This helps the layers set.
  10. Serve hot, and enjoy your delicious homemade lasagna!
